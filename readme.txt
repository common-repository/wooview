== Description ==

**WooView** is the best WooCommerce iPhone app designed to access your WooCommerce shop on the go, for **FREE**!

Simply install our free Wordpress plugin on your site then download the **WooView** iPhone app [here](http://appstore.com/wooview "Download WooView from the App Store"). That's it!

= Features =
* Watch your sales grow throughout the day with our simple dashboard,
* Change and update WooCommerce order status, customer notes, and order notes,
* View customer information and order details,
* View taxes and shipping information,
* Secure connection to your WooCommerce shop,
* Many more!

= Notes =
* This plugin requires the free WooView iPhone App,
* You must have Shop Manager or Administrator privileges to use our WooCommerce App WooView,
* Form more information about WooView please visit <http://www.wooviewapp.com>.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'wooview' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Download the **free** 'WooView' iOS app from the Apple App Store [here](http://appstore.com/wooview "Download WooView from the App Store").
